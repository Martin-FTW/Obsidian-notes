# Theorem 2.10 (Riemann condition)
Let $f:\clcl{a}{b}\to \mathbb{R}$ be bounded.
Then $f\in R\clcl{a}{b}$ iff  $\forall \varepsilon>0:\exists P:a=x_{0}<\cdots<x_{n}=b$ s.t. $U(f,P)-L(f,P)<\varepsilon$.
