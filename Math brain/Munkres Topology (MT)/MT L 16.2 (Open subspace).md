# Lemma 16.2 (Open subspace)
Let $Y$ be a subspace of $X$.
Let $U$ be open in $Y$.
Suppose $Y$ is open in $X$.
Then $U$ is open in $X$.
#### Proof
By definition, $\exists V$ open in $X$ s.t. $U=V\cap Y$.
Now $Y$ is open in $X$ and $V$ is open in $X$.
Hence $U=V\cap Y$ is open in $X$.
