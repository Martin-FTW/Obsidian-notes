# Theorem 6.11 (Adjoint properties)
Let $V$ be an inner product space and $T,U$ be linear operators on $V$.
Then
1. $(T+U)^{*}=T^{*}+U^{*}$
2. $(cT)^{*}=\conj{c}T^{*}$ for any $c\in F$
3. $(TU)^{*}=U^{*}T^{*}$
4. $T^{**}=T$
5. $I^{*}=I$
## Corollary
Let $T,U$ be $n\times n$ matrices.
Then
1. $(T+U)^{*}=T^{*}+U^{*}$
2. $(cT)^{*}=\conj{c}T^{*}$ for any $c\in F$
3. $(TU)^{*}=U^{*}T^{*}$
4. $T^{**}=T$
5. $I^{*}=I$
