# Definition (Matrix representation of a bilinear form)
Let $V$ be an $n$-dimensional vector space over a field $F$.
Let $\beta=\set{v_{i}}_{i=1}^{n}$ be a ordered basis.
Let $H\in \mathcal{B}(V)$
Let $A\in \mathcal{M}_{n}(F)$ be defined by $A_{ij}=H(v_{i},v_{j})$.
We call this matrix the matrix representation $H$ w.r.t. $\beta$.

We define $\psi_{\beta}:\mathcal{B}(V)\to \mathcal{M}_{n}(F)$ by $\psi_{\beta}(H)=[H(v_{i},v_{j})]_{n\times n}$.
