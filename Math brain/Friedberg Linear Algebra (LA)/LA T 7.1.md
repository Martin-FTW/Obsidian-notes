# Theorem 7.1
Let $T$ be a linear operator on a vector space $V$.
Let $\lambda$ be an eigenvalue of $T$.
Then
1. $K_{\lambda}$ is a $T$-invariatn subsapce of $V$ containing $E_{\lambda}$.
2. $\forall$ scalar $\mu \neq \lambda$, $(T-\mu I)_{K_{\lambda}}$ is 1-1.
